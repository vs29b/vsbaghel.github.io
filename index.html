    • About:

    I am a research scholar in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, IIT Indore, Indore, India. I am working in the Pattern Analysis & Machine Intelligence lab under the supervision of Dr. Surya Prakash. My research interest mainly includes Biometrics, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning.

      In my PhD thesis work, I am currently working in the area of biometric template protection. My work involves the development of secure and efficient techniques to protect the user template stored in the database as the databases are vulnerable to attack which can cause an identity theft of a user. Presently, I am working towards the developement of robust techniques to secure the fingerprint user template.

      I have also keen interest towards the Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques. In future, I have plans to work in these field which includes the contact less fingerprint recognition using Deep Learning, 3D fingerprint recognition, generation of synthetic biometric data by utilizing the generative deep learning models, and construction of 3D equivalent of 2D images.
